In our project, we will build a website that allows users to make smart, informed decisions based on recent news and stock trends via a compiled sentiment score. Our project will use a ChatGPT LLM that compares news and stock data stored in a database. The news articles will be accessed through a web scraper. This stock information will include computed statistics on price movement and news stories that correlate to the stock's performance. Once compared, our app will categorize how the stock performs, and recent news stories will help capture price movement not easily seen during standard stock analysis. Lastly, it is important to stress that it is up to the user to see what they want to do with their stocks based on our compiled analysis assessing market conditions.
