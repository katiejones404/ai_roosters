A cleaner system for understanding the market

From live sentiment scores to portfolio analytics, StockSense puts useful stock tools in one clean place.

Live Sentiment Scoring

FinBERT-powered analysis reads financial news and summarizes market sentiment so you can see how coverage is trending over time.

Portfolio Management

Add, buy, sell, and track positions with gain and loss tracking. Compare holdings side by side and export your data as CSV or PDF.

Smart Price Alerts

Set price-above or price-below thresholds for supported stocks and get notified when your target is reached.

AI News Summaries

GPT-generated summaries give you the context behind the latest headlines without forcing you to read every article.

Net Worth Tracker

Combine your portfolio with assets and liabilities for a complete financial picture, all in one dashboard.

What-If Analysis

Run historical scenario analysis on supported tickers to see what an investment would be worth today.

How the product works

StockSense connects news, prices, alerts, and portfolio data into a single workflow so the experience feels cohesive and deliberate.

01

Sign Up & Build Your Portfolio

Create an account and add supported stocks to your watchlist or portfolio. Import existing positions from CSV to get started faster.

02

AI Reads the News

Our pipeline ingests financial news, runs sentiment analysis, and rolls the results up to the stock level automatically.

03

Track, Analyze, and Act

View charts with sentiment context, set price alerts, read news summaries, and monitor your net worth in one place.

04

Export and Report

Download portfolio data, transaction history, and net worth snapshots as PDF or CSV whenever you need them.

Built for clarity at every step
